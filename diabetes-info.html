Diabetes (مرض السكر)

Diabetes, or diabetes mellitus, is a chronic condition associated with abnormally high levels of sugar (glucose) in the blood. Insulin, produced by the pancreas, lowers blood glucose. Absence or insufficient production of insulin, or an inability of the body to properly use insulin causes diabetes.

There are two main types of diabetes: Type 1 diabetes is an autoimmune disease that results in the body not producing insulin. Type 2 diabetes occurs when the body becomes resistant to insulin or when the pancreas does not produce enough insulin.

Tips for Managing Diabetes:

  • Monitor your blood sugar levels regularly.
  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in whole grains, fruits, and vegetables.
  • Exercise regularly to maintain a healthy weight and improve insulin sensitivity.
  • Take your insulin or diabetes medication as prescribed.
  • Avoid sugary foods and drinks that can spike your blood sugar levels.
  • Stay hydrated and drink plenty of water.
  • Consult with your healthcare provider regularly for check-ups.
